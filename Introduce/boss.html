The Boss

The Beginning

I lucked out and got a fabulous job right out of college at Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals based in Salt Lake City, Utah. I enjoyed 10+ years working in various communications, public relations and innovation positions. It was a joy to spend every day working to help children’s hospitals throughout the U.S. and Canada.

 

One of the highlights of this job was each year taking a child from each state to meet their senators and The President of The United States in Washington, D.C. It was an unparalleled experience to work with such amazing children who had overcome immense health challenges.

 

Another highlight was in 2011 CMN Hospitals made a bold move in the nonprofit world by creating a high-level, cross-functional team dedicated entirely to innovation. I was privileged to be a part of the team. My responsibilities included human-centered research, ideation, conceptualization, prototyping, test management and execution. I loved being introduced to the design thinking field and the power of user-centered design.

 

Also during this time I taught PR and Communications classes at my local community college. I loved teaching.

The Middle

The last six years, I took a brief break from full-time employment to fully immerse myself in the ultimate creative endeavor...raising three children.

 

During this time my husband and I started a niche real estate company called First Class Crashpads that accommodates pilots and flight attendants based in Chicago. We started out renting to 20 people and now we own three buildings and rent to 100 airline crews. We’ve also developed the strongest brand and web presence for this niche market in Chicago.

 

 

The Next Step

Owning a small business has been an eye-opening experience that has highlighted some communication opportunities in the small-business sector of the economy. To capitalize on those opportunities, I'm currently polishing my communication and design skills by pursuing a Graphic Design certificate. I'd like to help other small businesses with their communication, writing and design needs.

 

After finishing my certificate I've also contemplated pursuing another masters degree or PHD in Design Thinking or Communications.

 

I'm also interested in teaching again at the college level.
