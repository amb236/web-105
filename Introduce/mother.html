The Mother

Thankfully, my youngest child is now three and I feel like my perpetually-sleep-deprived days are somewhat behind me. Unfortunately, now my web development class keeps me up all night swearing at code.

 

My favorite thing about being a parent is answering questions. I love trying to explain the world in simple, creative ways.
