Definitions

Pattern
A regular arrangement of alternated or repeated elements such as shapes, lines, colors or motifs.
Contrast
The justaposition of different elements of design. For example, rough and smooth textures, dark and light values, in order to highlight their differences and/or create cisual interest or a focal point.
Emphasis
Special attention/importance given to one part of a work of art. Emphasis can be achieved through placement, contrast, colour, size, and repetition.
Balance
A feeling of balance results when the elements of design are arranged symmetrically or asymmetrically to create the impression of equality in weight or importance.
Porporation/Scale
The relationship between objects with respect to size, number, and so on, including the relation between parts of a whole.
