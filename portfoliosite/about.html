About Me

I'm a writer, designer, innovator and communicator. I'm passionate about helping meaningful organization's find their voice and communicate their mission. My most significant employment experience was working for 10+ years at an international children's healthcare charity in communications, public relations and innovation roles.

I took a brief break from full-time employment to fully immerse myself in the ultimate creative endeavor...raising three children.

During that time my husband and I started a small business. Owning a business was an eye-opening experience that illuminated some communication opportunities in that sector of the economy.

​

To capitalize on those opportunities I am currently polishing my communication and design skills by pursuing a Graphic Design Certificate at McHenry County College. My ultimate goal is to help small businesses and other meaningful organizations with their communication, writing and design needs.
