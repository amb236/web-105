The Castle

The most visual and stunning part of Edinburgh, Scotland, is it's iconic castle. The castle sits up on a large rocky hill right in the middle of the city. The castle has played a huge role in many of Scotland's most historic moments in history. It has been a royal residence since the 12th century. Mary Queen of Scots gave birth to her only son in the Castle.

When I visited the castle, I was blown away by how old everything was. Nothing in America has such ancient history. I loved the beautiful Great Hall and visiting "the dungeons". It is a remarkable place to visit and imagine all the incredible history of the Scotland area.
