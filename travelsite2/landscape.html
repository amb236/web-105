The Landscape

The landscape of Scotland is stunning. When you visit the castle, you can truly take in the amazing landscape that surrounds the urban city. It makes you want to get out of the city and go exploring. Which is exactly what we did.

We took a day-trip tour through the countryside and highlands of Scotland. It was as breathtaking as you can imagine. We saw highland cows, iconic movie sets, and endless green hills and valleys. Everything was incredibly green. The highlight of our countryside tour was a visit to Loch Ness. It was a quaint, beautiful town. Unfortunately, we didn't see Nessie, the Loch Ness monster, but maybe another day!
